Top Activities

Historic Monuments

Delhi's heritage trail lets you step back in time through stunning landmarks, from the towering Qutab Minar to the majestic gardens of Humayun's and Safdarjung tombs. You'll explore centuries of incredible architecture and royal history, ending with an evening walk along the grand lawns of India Gate.,

explore more about monuments

Bazaars & Culinary Treats

This activity combines retail therapy with iconic local food across New Delhi's famous shopping hubs. You can pick up handmade regional crafts at Dilli Haat, browse high-end boutiques and famous eateries in Khan Market, and grab a classic snack at the legendary Wenger’s in Connaught Place,

explore more about bazaars

Adventure Activities

Get your adrenaline pumping right in the heart of the city with thrills like wall climbing and zip-lining at the Indian Mountaineering Foundation (IMF) campus. For an extra rush, you can try outdoor rock climbing at Lado Sarai or join a guided sunrise cycling tour down empty, tree-lined boulevards.

explore more about adventures

Local Travel Experience

Navigating the city like a resident means jumping onto the modern, air-conditioned and fully automatic metro system to beat the surface traffic. Its clean, color-coded lines are incredibly easy to use, making it the fastest and most affordable way to hop between major tourist sights.

explore more about metro

Spiritual Landmarks

Experience the peaceful side of New Delhi by visiting its diverse sacred spaces, including the stunning Lotus Temple and the vibrant Birla Mandir. The highlight is visiting Bangla Sahib, where you can sit by the holy pool and see the massive community kitchen feeding thousands for free daily.,

explore more about temples

Museums & Public Parks

Unwind in the city's green spaces and interactive museums, like the massive sundials at Jantar Mantar or the beautiful Garden of Five Senses. You'll also dive into unique history by viewing old royal train carriages and catching a fun ride on the National Rail Museum's toy train.

explore more about museums
